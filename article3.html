CSS Tips for Beginners

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) allows you to style your HTML content. It's what makes websites look beautiful! You can change colors, fonts, layouts, and make your pages responsive for mobile and desktop.

Start by learning basic selectors, the box model, margins, padding, and positioning. Mastering Flexbox and Grid will make your layouts clean and adaptable.

Always keep your CSS organized — it will save you a lot of time as your projects grow!
